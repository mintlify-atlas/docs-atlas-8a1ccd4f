Welcome to the facility, recruit. This guide walks you through your first boot, setup, and orientation.

## Boot sequence

Urbanshade OS follows a realistic boot sequence every time you load the page:

| Step | Label | Description |
|------|-------|-------------|
| 1 | `POST` | Power-On Self Test |
| 2 | `BIOS` | Press `DEL` to enter BIOS settings |
| 3 | `BOOT` | Loading kernel |
| 4 | `INIT` | Starting services |
| 5 | `LOGIN` | User authentication |
| 6 | `DESKTOP` | Ready to use |

<Tip>
Press `DEL` during POST to enter BIOS settings. Press `F2` to enter Recovery Mode at any boot stage.
</Tip>

## Installation types

Choose your installation type when you first set up Urbanshade OS. You can always install more apps later from the App Store.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Minimal (~2 min)">
    Core system only. Fast installation for backup terminals and lightweight usage.

    **Included:**
    - Desktop Environment
    - Terminal
    - File Explorer
    - Calculator
    - Browser
    - App Store
    - Settings

    **Not included:** Facility Apps, Research Tools, Game Hub, DEF-DEV Console, all 70+ apps
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Standard (~5 min) ★">
    Recommended for most users. Includes facility tools and productivity apps.

    **Included:**
    - Everything in Minimal
    - Facility Apps

    **Not included:** Research Tools, Game Hub, DEF-DEV Console, all 70+ apps

    <Note>This is the recommended installation for most users.</Note>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Complete (~10 min)">
    Full installation with all applications, research modules, and developer tools.

    **Included:**
    - Everything in Standard
    - Research Tools
    - Game Hub
    - DEF-DEV Console
    - All 70+ apps
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

After installation, the Out of Box Experience (OOBE) wizard guides you through personalizing your workstation.

<Tip>
You can change any of these settings later in the Settings app.
</Tip>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Welcome">

  </Step>
  <Step title="Account creation">
    Set your username and password. This is a simulation — do not use real credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Theme selection">

  </Step>
  <Step title="Privacy settings">
    Configure data and sync preferences.
  </Step>

    Review all settings and complete the installation.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Login screen

After OOBE completes, you will see the login screen on every subsequent visit.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select your account">
    Click your user account tile on the login screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your password">
    Type your password in the field that appears.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log in">
    Press `Enter` or click the arrow button to access the desktop.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
This is a simulation. All data is stored in your browser's `localStorage`. Do not use real passwords.
</Warning>

To lock the screen later, press `Shift+L` from the desktop.
