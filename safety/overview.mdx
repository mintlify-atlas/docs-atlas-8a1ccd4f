## How we protect you

Protection is layered — each layer catches what the previous one might miss.

<Steps>
  <Step title="NAVI AI Monitor">
    Automated threat detection watches for spam, abuse, and suspicious patterns around the clock. NAVI never sleeps and responds in seconds.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Rate limiting">
    Message and action rate limits prevent spam floods and automated abuse before they can affect other users.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Badge verification">
    Badges are rendered by the system itself — they cannot be typed, faked, or impersonated. If someone claims to be staff, check for their badge.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Human moderation">
    Real admins review reports, handle appeals, and make final decisions. Automated systems assist but do not replace human judgment.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Autonomous response">
    In emergencies, NAVI can auto-warn, issue temporary bans, or trigger a full lockdown without waiting for a human to act.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Core safety principles

The following principles guide every safety and moderation decision in Urbanshade OS.

**Badges are ground truth.** Any claim of being staff is only valid if the correct system badge is visible on the account. No badge means no staff status — period.

**Staff will never ask for your password.** No one from Urbanshade OS — including the creator (Aswd) and all admins — will ever request your password. If someone does, they are attempting to steal your account.

**Reports are taken seriously.** Every report is reviewed by a human admin. However, filing false reports wastes everyone's time and may result in action against your own account.

**Actions escalate progressively.** Moderation follows a severity scale. Minor violations result in warnings before more serious action is taken. Permanent bans are reserved for the most severe or repeated violations.

**All moderation is logged.** Every action taken by an admin is recorded with a timestamp, the admin's identity, and a reason. This creates accountability on both sides.

## Reporting flow

When you submit a report, here is what happens:

<Steps>
  <Step title="You report">
    Message an admin directly through the Messages app, or use the in-app report function if available.
  </Step>
  <Step title="NAVI filters">
    Automated systems check if the reported behavior matches known patterns to help admins prioritize.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Admin reviews">
    A human admin reviews the report, examines the evidence, and considers context before acting.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Action taken">
    If a violation is confirmed, the appropriate moderation action is applied — from a warning to a ban.
  </Step>
  <Step title="You are notified">
    The reporting user receives a response once the review is complete.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Moderation severity scale

Actions escalate based on the severity and frequency of violations.

| Level | Action | Duration | Description |
|-------|--------|----------|-------------|
| 1 | Warning | Permanent record | Informal notice. No restrictions. Accumulates toward bans. |
| 2 | Temp mute | 1h – 24h | Chat and message privileges temporarily revoked. |
| 3 | Temp ban | 1h – 30 days | Full access revoked for a set duration. |
| 4 | Permanent ban | Indefinite | Account permanently restricted. Appeal is possible. |
| 5 | Lockdown | Until resolved | System-wide emergency restriction. Affects all users. |
